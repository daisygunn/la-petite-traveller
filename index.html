Welcome to La Petite Traveller

The go-to guide for any backpacker!

I have been very fortunate to spend a lot of my adult life exploring our beautiful planet. Whilst there are plenty of travel blogs and guides out there, I found that when researching my first big trip there just wasn't enough out there about backpacking on a budget that didn't include missing out on half of the country! I want to give you, my readers the best tips that I've learnt along the way so that you can make the most of every second & every penny that you have to make life-long memories.
